Hello World!

1/22/16

Both groups building ballon tower.

Today was the First day of our Advanced Computer Science ellective, and I enjoyed it.

We where put to make a ballon tower today. There where some basic rules to this but nothing to complicated. Just mostly like not being able to use past 25 ballons or not being able to tape it on to the table.

After building the tower my "group" built a tower that was about 57 inches and the other "group" built it up to be about 17 inches, if i remember correctly. After the measuring of the ballon towers we had a chat with Mr. Yoho, our teacher in this class. He questioned us about the rules. He said that no where in the rules did it say that we had to work in groups, but we still did . He pointed this out because he wants us to start thinking out of the box when it comes to rules. After the conversation our teacher let both of the groups work together. When we all worked together we managed to build a tower that was about 86 inch tower.

I enjoyed the first day of this elective.
